About UniLink

Connecting students, empowering educators, and transforming education

Our Story

UniLink was founded with a simple vision: to democratize education by creating a marketplace where students can share their knowledge and help their peers succeed. We believe that every student has valuable insights to share, and every learner deserves access to high-quality study materials at affordable prices.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Empower students to learn better by providing access to quality study materials created by their peers, while enabling educators to earn from sharing their knowledge.

Our Vision

To become the leading peer-to-peer educational marketplace across East Africa, where knowledge flows freely and education is accessible to everyone.

Our Values

We value integrity, accessibility, quality, and community. Everything we do is guided by our commitment to these core principles.

Core Values

Integrity

We maintain honesty and transparency in all our operations and treat every user with respect.

Accessibility

Education should be affordable and accessible to everyone, regardless of their background.

Quality

We are committed to maintaining high standards of content and service for all users.

Community

We foster a supportive community where students help each other succeed.
