Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about UniLink

Buyer Questions

What is the activation fee?

The activation fee is a one-time 50 KES payment required to access the marketplace as a buyer or seller. This fee helps maintain platform quality and security.

How long can I access purchased notes?

You have access to purchased notes for 30 days from the purchase date. During this period, you can download the notes up to 10 times. After expiration, you can purchase access again.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa and card payments. Both are processed securely and instantly. Your transaction is encrypted and protected.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer refunds within 7 days if the notes don't match the description. Contact our support team with your purchase proof and reason for refund.

How do I download my purchased notes?

After purchase, go to "My Purchases" or "Downloads" in your dashboard. Click the download button on any active purchase. You can download up to 10 times per purchase.

Can I share purchased notes with friends?

No, purchased notes are for your personal use only. Sharing copyrighted content violates our terms. Each user must purchase their own copy.

Seller Questions

How do I become a seller?

Create an account, complete your profile, pay the 50 KES activation fee, and start uploading notes. Your account must be verified by our team before you can upload content.

What format should my notes be in?

We accept PDF, DOCX, TXT, JPG, and PNG formats. Make sure your notes are clear, well-organized, and free of errors. Quality is important!

How much can I earn as a seller?

You keep 80% of the selling price. For example, if you sell notes for 100 KES, you earn 80 KES after our 20% commission.

When will I receive my earnings?

Earnings are available after 30 days of a sale. You can withdraw to M-Pesa or bank account anytime. Minimum withdrawal is 100 KES.

How long does content approval take?

Usually 24-48 hours. Our team reviews content for quality, accuracy, and compliance. You'll receive email notification once approved.

What if my content is rejected?

You'll receive detailed feedback about why it was rejected. Fix the issues and resubmit. Common reasons include formatting issues, copyright concerns, or inaccurate content.

General Questions

Is my data secure on UniLink?

Yes, we use encryption and secure servers to protect your data. All payments are processed through secure gateways. We never share your information with third parties.

How can I contact customer support?

Visit our Contact page or email support@unilink.ke. We typically respond within 24 hours. For urgent issues, call our support hotline.

Do you have a mobile app?

Our website is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. Native mobile apps for iOS and Android are coming soon!

What universities do you support?

UniLink supports all universities in Kenya and East Africa. You can specify your university during registration and find notes specific to your institution.

How do you ensure content quality?

We have a moderation team that reviews all content. Buyers can also rate and review notes. Poor-quality content is removed and sellers are warned.
