How It Works

Simple steps to start learning or earning on UniLink

For Buyers

Follow these simple steps to find and purchase study materials

1

Create Account

Sign up with your email, university, and course details to get started on UniLink.

2

Pay Activation Fee

Pay a one-time activation fee of 50 KES to unlock the marketplace and start browsing.

3

Browse & Search

Search by course, unit, or keywords. Filter by category and sort by rating or price.

4

Preview Notes

View sample content, seller ratings, and reviews before deciding to purchase.

5

Make Payment

Pay securely using M-Pesa or card. Instant confirmation and access granted.

6

Download & Review

Download your notes, access for 30 days, and leave reviews to help others.

For Sellers

Start earning by sharing your quality study materials

1

Register

Create an account and set up your seller profile with university and expertise details.

2

Verify Account

Complete account verification and pay the 50 KES activation fee to become a seller.

3

Upload Notes

Upload your study materials with detailed descriptions, pricing, and course details.

4

Get Approved

Our team reviews your content for quality and compliance. Takes 24-48 hours usually.
